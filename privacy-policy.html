Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

The Bot collects the following information to provide its services:

  • User IDs: To track counting progress, leaderboard rankings, and award coins.
  • Server IDs: To manage counting games and leaderboards for each server.
  • Messages in the Counting Channel: To process counting numbers and enforce game rules.
  • Coins and Purchased Commands: To track user balances and purchased commands.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Counting Game: To track the current number, enforce rules, and award roles based on milestones.
  • Leaderboards: To display server and global leaderboards.
  • Coins and Economy: To manage user balances and allow users to purchase commands.
  • Fun Commands: To enable interaction commands like `/hug`, `/punch`, `/kiss`, etc.

3. Data Storage

All data (user IDs, server IDs, counts, coins, etc.) is stored in a secure MySQL database. Data is retained as long as the Bot is active in your server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The Bot operates within Discord's ecosystem and complies with Discord's Developer Terms of Service.

5. Your Rights

You can view your data (e.g., counts, coins) using commands like `/total_count` and `/coin`. You can also request the deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including using secure database connections and limiting access to authorized personnel only.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of significant changes via the Bot or our support server.
